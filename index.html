How to play:

  • Roll the dice as many times as you want.
  • The dice total adds to the current score.
  • If you roll a 1 your current score goes to 0 and your turn is over.
  • You can end your turn at any time by clicking "Hold". Your current score will be added to your saved score.
  • The first player to reach 100 points in the saved score wins!
